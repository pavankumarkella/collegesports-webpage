SPORTS

Sports provide a structured platform for goal setting, motivating students to work towards personal and team objectives. These activities also encourage healthy lifestyle habits, including proper nutrition, regular exercise, and avoiding harmful substances, promoting lifelong well-being. Better academic performance is often associated with sports participation, as students apply the discipline and time management skills they develop in sports to their studies.
